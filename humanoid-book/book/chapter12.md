# Chapter 12: Current Humanoid Platforms Comparison

The field of humanoid robotics is vibrant and rapidly evolving, with new platforms emerging and existing ones being continuously improved. This chapter provides a technical comparison of some of the most significant humanoid robots in the 2025 landscape, based on publicly available specifications and research.

## Boston Dynamics Atlas

Atlas is arguably the most famous humanoid robot, known for its incredible dynamic capabilities, including running, jumping, and performing complex gymnastics. We will examine:
- **Actuation**: Its powerful hydraulic actuation system.
- **Control**: The model-predictive control strategies that enable its agility.
- **Sensing**: The role of its perception system in navigating unstructured terrain.

## Agility Robotics Digit

Digit is a bipedal robot designed for logistics and package handling tasks. Its key features include:
- **Efficiency**: A design optimized for long-duration operation in human environments.
- **Locomotion**: A unique bird-like leg configuration.
- **Manipulation**: Arms and hands designed for grasping and carrying objects.

## Tesla Optimus

Tesla's entry into the humanoid robotics space has generated significant interest. We will analyze what is publicly known about Optimus, focusing on:
- **AI-driven Approach**: Its connection to Tesla's work in autonomous driving and AI.
- **Actuation and Hardware**: The shift towards electric actuators and its implications.
- **Manufacturing at Scale**: Tesla's vision for mass-producing humanoid robots.
